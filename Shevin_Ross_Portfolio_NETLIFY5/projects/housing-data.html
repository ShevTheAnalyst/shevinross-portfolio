Key Findings

Insights from regression modelling and feature analysis

Finding 1: Square footage and grade (construction quality) are the top two predictors of price, together accounting for 62% of variance explained by the Random Forest model.
Finding 2: Waterfront properties command a 45–80% price premium on average, making view/location the single highest-value binary feature in the dataset.
Finding 3: The Random Forest Regressor outperformed Linear Regression (R²: 0.884 vs 0.702), demonstrating the benefit of ensemble methods for non-linear real estate pricing data.
Model Used: Random Forest Regressor with 200 estimators, max depth 15, trained on 80% of data. Mean Absolute Error of $42,000 on the test set.
