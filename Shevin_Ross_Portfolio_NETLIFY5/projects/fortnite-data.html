Key Findings

Insights from statistical analysis of the Fortnite dataset

Finding 1: Players with a KD ratio above 3.0 win 28% of their matches on average — nearly 4x the global average of 6.8%, confirming that kill efficiency is strongly correlated with wins.
Finding 2: Shotguns and assault rifles account for 68% of all eliminations, making them the dominant weapon classes regardless of player skill level.
Finding 3: Match duration has decreased from an average of 25 minutes in Season 1 to 22 minutes by Season 8, reflecting faster-paced meta shifts and storm timing changes.
