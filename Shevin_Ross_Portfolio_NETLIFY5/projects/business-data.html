Key Findings & Recommendations

Insights derived from end-to-end business data analysis

Finding 1: Electronics and Apparel account for 54% of total revenue but only 38% of SKUs — indicating these two categories punch significantly above their weight and deserve prioritised investment.
Finding 2: Rent and payroll constitute 71% of total operating costs. A 10% reduction in overhead (e.g. remote work hybrid, renegotiated leases) could add approximately $48K to net profit annually.
Finding 3: Social media drives 42% of new customer acquisition at the lowest cost-per-acquisition ($12 vs $38 for paid ads), suggesting reallocation of marketing budget away from traditional channels.
Recommendation: Focus Q3–Q4 inventory investment on Electronics and Apparel, reduce paid advertising spend by 30%, and explore remote-hybrid models to cut facility costs.
