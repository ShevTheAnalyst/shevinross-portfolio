I'm Shevin Ross — a Developer, Web Designer, Junior Data Analyst, and aspiring Game Developer from Port of Spain, Trinidad & Tobago. I blend creative design with technical thinking to build digital products that look great and perform even better.

Currently pursuing a BSc in Computer Science at UWI. I'm passionate about game design and am actively developing an original IP — a 4-element Battle Royale built in Unreal Engine 5 with C++. I believe games are the most powerful form of interactive storytelling, and building them is where my creativity and technical skills fully collide.
